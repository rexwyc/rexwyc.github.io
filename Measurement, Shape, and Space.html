Measurement, Shape, and Space

📐 Study Formulas Reference Sheet ▼
Mensuration (Area & Volume)
  • • Volume of Cylinder: V = π * r² * h
  • • Curved Surface Area of Cone: A = π * r * l
  • • Volume of Pyramid: V = (1/3) * Base Area * h
  • • Volume of Sphere: V = (4/3) * π * r³
  • • Total S.A. of Hemisphere: A = 3 * π * r²
  • • Total S.A. of Cylinder: A = 2πr(r + h)
  • • Arc Length: L = 2 * π * r * (θ / 360°)
  • • Sector Area: A = π * r² * (θ / 360°)
Coordinate Geometry
  • • Distance Formula: d = √((x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²)
  • • Midpoint Formula: M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)
  • • Slope (m): m = (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁)
  • • Perpendicular Slopes: m₁ * m₂ = -1
  • • Circle Standard Form: (x - h)² + (y - k)² = r²
  • • Circle General Form: x² + y² + Dx + Ey + F = 0
      Center = (-D/2, -E/2)
Trigonometry & Shapes
  • • Identity 1: sin²θ + cos²θ = 1
  • • Identity 2: 1 + tan²θ = sec²θ
  • • Triangle Area (SAS): Area = 0.5 * a * b * sin C
  • • Sine Rule: a / sin A = b / sin B = c / sin C
  • • Interior Angles Sum: (n - 2) * 180°
  • • Regular Exterior Angle: 360° / n
  • • Space Diagonal: d = √(l² + w² + h²)
