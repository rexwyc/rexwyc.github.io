Using Connectives Effectively

Connectives (also called transition words or cohesive devices) are essential for creating smooth, logical flow in writing. They help readers follow your argument and understand the relationships between your ideas.

Tips for using connectives:

  • Choose connectives that accurately reflect the relationship between ideas
  • Vary your connectives to avoid repetition
  • Place connectives at the beginning of sentences or clauses for maximum impact
  • Ensure the connective matches the formality of your writing
