📱 Weakness: Phone Distraction

The Impact: Resulted in non-stop social media scrolling, forgetting work, and losing track of time.

  • Used a blocker app to restrict social media access.

The Outcome: Tough at first, but led to better concentration, higher efficiency, and finishing work in less time.
