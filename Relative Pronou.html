Which

Refers to animals, things

"The book which I read"

That

Refers to people, animals, things

"The car that I bought"

Key Rules for Relative Pronouns

Who/Whom: Use for people only. "Who" = subject, "Whom" = object.
Which: Use for animals and things only (never for people).
That: Use for people, animals, OR things. More common in defining clauses.
