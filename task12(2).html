Hitler Adolf Hitler (1889-1945) was the dictator of Nazi Germany from 1933 to 1945. He rose to power through extreme nationalism, anti-Semitism, and propaganda, initiating World War II and the Holocaust, which led to the genocide of six million Jews. His aggressive policies caused immense destruction before his suicide in 1945.
